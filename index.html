🔔 Notifications

Daily reminder
A gentle nudge to begin your practice — most reliable once Still Waters is installed as an app
Reminder time
📱 iPhone (Safari)
  1. Open this site in Safari (not Chrome — this only works in Safari on iPhone)
  2. Tap the Share icon (square with an arrow, at the bottom of the screen)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top right
  5. Open Still Waters from your home screen icon from now on — not the browser
  6. Come back here, toggle Daily reminder on, and allow notifications when asked
🤖 Android (Chrome)
  1. Open this site in Chrome
  2. Tap the ⋮ three-dot menu in the top right
  3. Tap "Install app" (sometimes shown as "Add to Home screen")
  4. Confirm by tapping "Install"
  5. Open Still Waters from its new app icon going forward
  6. Toggle Daily reminder on below and allow notifications when asked
💻 Desktop (Chrome / Edge / Brave)
  1. Look in the address bar — there's usually a small install icon (a computer screen with a ⊕ or ↓)
  2. Click it, then click "Install"
  3. If you don't see it, open the ⋮ menu → look for "Install Still Waters..."
  4. Toggle Daily reminder on below and allow notifications when asked
Reminders work best once installed and kept running in the background rather than fully closed — this is a limitation of browsers in general, not something specific to Still Waters.

Still Waters
Mindfulness Planner

Still Waters was born from a simple belief: that the most profound transformation begins not with grand gestures, but in the quiet moments you carve out for yourself each day. We created a space that feels as personal and unhurried as a handwritten letter — somewhere your thoughts can settle, your habits can take root, and your inner life can be tended with the same care you give to everything else.

Our Mission

To make mindfulness feel accessible, personal, and genuinely yours — not a rigid routine to perform, but a living practice that grows with you. We believe everyone deserves a space to pause, reflect, and return to themselves, no matter how full their day. Still Waters is that space: private, gentle, and always here when you need it.
